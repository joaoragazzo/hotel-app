About the Hotel Hotel

Welcome to Hotel Hotel, a haven of luxury and comfort located in the heart of Jacarta. Blending timeless elegance with modern amenities, our hotel promises an unforgettable stay for all guests.

From the moment of arrival, you will be enveloped in our warm hospitality and attentive service. Each of our rooms and suites has been meticulously designed to offer the utmost in comfort and style, featuring a serene color palette, exquisite furnishings, and all the contemporary amenities you could wish for.

Services Offered:

  • 1. Accommodation: Our rooms and suites are a blend of luxury and comfort, each designed to provide a peaceful retreat with all the modern amenities, including high-speed Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, and luxurious bedding.

  • 2. Dining: Guests can indulge in culinary delights at our on-site restaurant, which offers a diverse menu of local and international cuisine, crafted by our expert chefs. Our hotel also features a cozy bar, perfect for sipping on cocktails or enjoying a casual meal.

  • 3. Wellness and Fitness: Our state-of-the-art fitness center is open 24/7, allowing guests to maintain their fitness routine. Additionally, our spa offers a serene environment for relaxation, with treatments ranging from massages to beauty therapies.

  • 4. Business Facilities: For our business travelers, we provide well-equipped meeting rooms and a business center with all the necessary tools and technologies for a productive work environment.

  • 5. Concierge Services: Our knowledgeable concierge team is at your service to assist with anything from travel arrangements and tour bookings to restaurant recommendations and reservations.

  • 6. Event Hosting: Whether it's a business conference, a wedding, or any other event, our versatile event spaces and dedicated planning team ensure a seamless and successful event.

  • 7. Additional Amenities: We offer a range of other amenities, including room service, laundry and dry cleaning, airport shuttle service, and ample parking.

History:

Nestled in the heart of Jacarta, the story of Hotel Hotel is one of heritage, innovation, and a commitment to excellence. Since our inception in 2001, our hotel has been a symbol of the unique blend of historical charm and contemporary luxury.

The hotel was founded by Joseph Elias, a visionary with a passion for hospitality and a deep love for Jacarta. Originally built as a private mansion, the building was transformed into a hotel to share the beauty of Jacarta with visitors from around the world.

Over the years, Hotel Hotel has seen numerous renovations and expansions, each carefully designed to preserve the building's original character while incorporating modern amenities and design elements.
